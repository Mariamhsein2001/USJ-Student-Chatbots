##GPA-Based Course Load:
- GPA ≥ 16: Allow challenging or advanced courses, including electives or specialization courses. (30-36 credits)
- 14 ≤ GPA < 16: Maintain a moderate workload, focusing on core courses with 1–2 electives. (30-36 credits)
- 12 ≤ GPA < 14: Focus on core and easier courses only. Avoid advanced electives. (30-36 credits)
- GPA < 12: Take only essential or repeated core courses. No new electives.(Under Probation: 30 credits max )
## Prioritize courses as follows from high to low priority:
    1. Required courses from previous semester
    2. UE Obligatoires 
    3. UE Obligatoires pour l'option
    4. Optionnelle Fermée
    5. Optionnels Ouvertes
- Consider course weights for course selection.
## Constraints:
- Avoid courses similar to previously failed ones unless mandatory.
- Do not exceed the GPA-appropriate credit limit.
## Scholarship / Financial Aid:
- If the student has a scholarship or financial aid, prioritize maintaining high grades.
- Recommend safer course loads and courses with high success potential.
## Student Interests:
- Prefer courses aligned with the student’s interests or specialization.
- Use interests to break ties between courses with the same priority level.
